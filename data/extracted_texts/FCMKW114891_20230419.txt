Statement of availability of Universal registration document 
 
Neuilly-sur-Seine, April 19, 2023 
 
 
 
 
 
Filing of the 2022 Universal registration document 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Gaumont announces that on April 19, 2023, it filed its 2022 Universal registration document to the Autorité des 
Marchés Financiers (the French financial markets authority). 
 
The Universal registration document is available free of charge to the public at Gaumont's registered office, 30 
avenue Charles de Gaulle, 92200 Neuilly-sur-Seine, France, upon request and can also be consulted on the 
company's website (www.gaumont.com) and the Autorité des Marchés Financiers website (www.amf-france.org).
