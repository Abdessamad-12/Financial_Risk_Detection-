ABOUT DASSAULT AVIATION:  
 
With over 10,000 military and civil aircraft (including 2,500 Falcons) delivered in more than 90 countries over the last 
century, Dassault Aviation has built up expertise recognized worldwide in the design, development, sale and support of 
all types of aircraft, ranging from the Rafale fighter, to the high-end Falcon family of business jets, military drones and 
space systems. In 2022, Dassault Aviation reported revenues of €6.9 billion. The company has 12,770 employees.
